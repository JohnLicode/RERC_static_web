About Us

The Research Ethics Review Committee (RERC) at Palawan State University is dedicated to ensuring that all research conducted within the university adheres to the highest ethical standards. Our mission is to promote ethical research practices, protect the rights and welfare of research participants, and foster a culture of integrity in research.
